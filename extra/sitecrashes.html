Why some sites crash upon opening

It's because DeskUtility uses an iframe to display a special version of Google.

Some sites use scripts to stop them from loading in an iframe. this is to prevent things like hacks, security bugs, DDoSing, etc.

Google does this too! I just used a special version of Google to do this that does not stop you.

So now you know why some sites are not loading. Anyways, click "Back To Google" to close this message and carry on!
